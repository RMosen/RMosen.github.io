About

The native Fremen of Arrakis (also known as Dune) have been traveling the desert upon the backs of the great sand worms for generations. Now, by kind permission of Emperor Paul Maud’Dib Atreides, you can take part in this ancient Fremen tradition! You will spend the day riding on the back of one of the Great Old Men of the Desert, guided by a trained Fremen worm-rider!
