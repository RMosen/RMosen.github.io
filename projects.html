I made this guitar in 2019. I built it from a kit that I bought on ebay. The neck and body were pre-cut, but I had to handle cutting out the pick-guard, staining and finishing the body and neck, as well as generally assembling the piece.

I haven't really learned how to play it very well, but from what I can tell it does play well, if that makes sense.
My girlfriend and I made this batch of ginger-beer around the end of 2019. We had made some homebrew before, but I think that this one was the best we’ve made. We named this ginger-beer “Forbidden Rat Barrel” because we wanted to.

The Ingredients are: Water, Sugar, Ginger, Orange, Cloves, Cinnamon, Tabasco Sauce, and Yeast. We were going for a combination of Christmas and Summer flavors when developing the recipe, and I think it turned out very well.
